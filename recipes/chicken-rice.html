Chicken Rice

Description

This recipe is a chicken, rice, and broccoli bowl. It is a simple, nutritional lunch with 47g of protein and 990 calories.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh - 6oz
  • Jasmine Rice - 1 cup
  • Raw Broccoli - 50g
  • Avocado Oil - 3 tsp
  • Water - 1.5 cup

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400
  2. Prepare chicken thighs on baking sheet
  3. Bake for 20 minutes or until internal temperature exceeds 175 degrees
  4. Combine rice and 1.5 cups of water in a bowl
  5. Microwave rice for 5 minutes on 100 power
  6. Reduce power to 50 and microwave for 10 minutes
  7. Toss broccoli into air fryer for 5 minutes on 390
  8. Combine foods and add any additional sauces
  9. Enjoy!
