French Toast

Description

This recipe is a simple 5 minute french toast. A nice treat with 34g of protein and 603 calories.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 4 Honey Wheat Bread Slices
  • 20g Peanut Butter Spread

Steps

  1. Whisk 3 eggs in a bowl
  2. Dip 4 slices of bread into egg bowl, ensuring all sides are covered
  3. Pan cook slices on medium heat
  4. Flip slices when golden brown
  5. Remove from heat and spread peanut butter on all slices
  6. Enjoy!
