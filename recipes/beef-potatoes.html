Beef & Potatoes

Description

This recipe is a beef, potato, and broccoli plate. It is a simple, nutritional lunch or dinner with 30g of protein and 500 calories.

Ingredients

  • Organic Ground Beef - 4oz
  • Potatoes - 300g
  • Broccoli - 100g

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef on a pan on medium heat
  2. Flip when brown
  3. Remove from heat when all brown or internal temperature is 185 degrees
  4. Slice potatoes into wedges
  5. Soak potatoes in ice water for 10 minutes
  6. Pat potatoes dry then air fry on 390 for 20 minutes
  7. Air fry broccoli on 390 for 5 minutes
  8. Plate food and add sauces such as hot sauce
  9. Enjoy!
